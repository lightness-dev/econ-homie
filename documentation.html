Documentation for Econ Homie Discord Economy Bot

Created by Lightness Development

1. Introduction

Welcome to Econ Homie, Discord's Ultimate Economy Bot, an economy bot for Discord that brings an entertaining twist to your community interactions. It features various fun commands, and users can earn, game, and spend virtual currency known as "Dollaridoos". It's important to remember that all currency, real estate, stocks, and crypto inside this virtual economy hold no real-world monetary value and are generated and managed solely by Econ Homie.

2. Commands

General Commands

  • help: Displays the help menu with all the functions of the bot.
  • about: Displays information about Econ Homie and its creator.
  • invite: Provides a link to invite Econ Homie to your server.
  • documentation: Provides a link to Econ Homie's documentation.
  • vote: Displays information on how to vote for Econ Homie.
  • ping: Gets the current bot latency.
  • createaccount: Creates an account for the command author and starts them with 100 Dollaridoos.
  • premium: Upgrade your account or check your premium membership status.
  • funds: Shows information about purchasing Dollaridoos.
  • settings: Modify your bot settings.
  • profile [user]: Displays the profile of a user. If no user is mentioned, displays the profile of the command author.
  • balance [user]: Shows the balance of a user. If no user is mentioned, displays the balance of the command author.
  • bank [user]: Shows the bank account info of a user. If no user is mentioned, shows the info of the command author.
  • inventory [user]: Displays the inventory of a user. If no user is mentioned, displays the inventory of the command author.
  • portfolio [user]: Shows the stock portfolio of a user. If no user is mentioned, displays the portfolio of the command author.
  • wallet [user]: Shows the crypto wallet of a user. If no user is mentioned, shows the crypto wallet of the command author.
  • property [user]: Display the property portfolio of the mentioned user. If no user is mentioned, it will display your own property portfolio.
  • refer [referrer_id]: Refer someone to Econ Homie using your Discord ID.
  • trade [user]: Allows trading items and/or Dollaridoos with another user.
  • shop: Displays a list of items available for purchase.
  • buy [item] [amount]: Purchase an item from the shop.
  • sell [item] [amount]: Sell an item to the shop.
  • daily: Get a random amount of Dollaridoos.
  • leaderboard: Displays the top users with the highest accumulated Dollaridoos.
  • transfer [user]: Transfers money or items from your balance/inventory to another user's balance/inventory.
  • withdraw [amount]: Withdraws an amount from your bank account.
  • deposit [amount]: Deposits an amount into your bank account.
  • stats: Displays the winning percentages and potential earnings for each game.
  • eightball [question]: Ask the Magic 8-Ball a question and receive a random response.
  • currency [base_currency] [target_currency]: Get the current conversion rate between two specified currencies. It also shows the change in the conversion rate for the day.

Earn Money Commands

  • lottery: Display information about PowerEcon lottery.
  • buyTicket [amount]: Buy tickets for the lottery.
  • business: Start your own unique business, view your own stats on your business and view other people's businesses.
  • realestate: Manage your real estate portfolio. Use $realestate view to view your properties, $realestate buy [type] to buy a new property, $realestate sell [property_id] to sell a property, $realestate rent [put/remove/collect] [property_id] to manage the rent of your properties, and $realestate shop to view available property types and their prices.
  • job: Manage your job.
  • rob [user]: Attempt to rob the mentioned user.
  • hunt or hunting: Go hunting and catch animals for rewards.
  • fish or fishing: Go fishing and catch fish for rewards.
  • beg: Beg for some money.
  • doubleornothing [amount]: Double the amount you put in or lose it all.
  • slots [amount]: Play the slots.
  • diceduel [bet] [user]: Roll dice against another player or The Bot.
  • tictactoe [bet] [user]: Play a game of tic tac toe against another player.
  • connectfour [bet] [user]: Play a game of Connect 4 against another player.
  • cardwar [bet] [user]: Play a game of Card War against another player.
  • wordscramble: Unscramble a word and win or lose 100 Dollaridoos!
  • race [amount] [animal]: Bet on a racing animal.
  • scratch [bet]: Buy a scratch ticket for a chance to win big!

Premium Commands

  • blackjack [amount]: Play a game of blackjack.
  • rockpaperscissors [amount]: Play a game of rock-paper-scissors with the bot.
  • stock [symbol]: Displays information about a stock.
  • buyStock [symbol] [quantity]: Buy stocks.
  • sellStock [symbol] [quantity]: Sell stocks.
  • updateStock: Display recent updates for your stocks!
  • crypto [cryptoCurrency]: Displays information about a cryptocurrency.
  • buyCrypto [symbol] [quantity]: Buy cryptocurrencies.
  • sellCrypto [symbol] [quantity]: Sell cryptocurrencies.
  • updateCrypto: Update the price of a cryptocurrency in your portfolio.
  • setProfileImage [background_url] or [reset]: Sets the profile image background for the command author. Use a 'background_url' to set a custom image or use 'reset' to reset it to the default background.
  • scam: Attempt to scam the mentioned user. If successful, you will receive a random amount of money, ranging from 20% to 30% of their current balance. The success rate of this command is fixed at 70%. Remember, this command is exclusive for premium members. Unsuccessful scams do not lead to fines, but be aware that users will be notified when a scam attempt is made against them.

Administrator Commands

  • setup: Setup Econ Homie in your server exactly how you want it by changing channels it can be used in, what prefix Econ Homie responds to, set up your serverShop, and more.
  • giveaway: Create a giveaway in your server (preferably with Dollaridoos)! To use this command you must be an approved giveaway creator with Econ Homie to generate Dollaridoos, or the Dollaridoos in the giveaway must be from yours or another person in charge of the giveaways account. You may use something like transfer to give the reward. To become one use about to contact us!

3. Cooldowns

Each command has a specific cooldown period to ensure fair play:

  • daily: 24 hours
  • rob and scam: 30 minutes
  • double or nothing: 15 seconds
  • racing, beg: 10 minutes (3 hours if caught begging)
  • scratch card: 3 minutes
  • slots and blackjack: 30 seconds
  • dice duel: 5 minutes
  • scramble: 4 hours
  • hunting: 2 hours
  • fishing: 1 hour

6. Referral System

Refer your friends to Econ Homie using the $refer command and earn escalating rewards for each successful referral! With this command, you refer someone by providing them with your Discord ID. They can then claim the referral by using the $refer command followed by your Discord ID (for example, $refer <Your_Discord_ID>). The new referral system has several features and requirements:

  • Rewards: The new referral system works on a tiered reward basis. The more friends you refer, the higher your reward will be! The bonus Dollaridoos for each referral increase according to the following tiers: 100, 150, 200, 300, 400, 600, 800, 1200. For example, if you refer 1 friend, you get 100 Dollaridoos; for 2 friends referred, you get 150 Dollaridoos each, and so on. Once you've reached 8 referrals and above, you will get 1200 Dollaridoos for each referral.
  • Referral bonus for the referred friend: The referred friend will always receive a bonus of 250 Dollaridoos.
  • Requirements: To refer someone, you need to have an Econ Homie account and have been a member of Discord for at least sixty days. Note that you can only be referred once.
  • Referral data: You can check your referral data, such as who referred you, how many users you have referred, how much you have earned from referrals, and how much you will earn from the next referral.

Note that you can't refer yourself and your referred friend should not have already been referred by someone else. Also, the Discord account of the referred friend should be at least sixty days old. If you don't have an Econ Homie account yet, use the $createaccount command to create one.

Once a referral is successful, you and your referred friend will be notified, and the referral bonus will be added to your accounts. The system will also update your referral status for future reference.
